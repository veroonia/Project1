At NileWay Rentals, transparency and customer trust are at the heart of our policies.

We’re committed to providing you with a safe and transparent rental experience throughout Egypt!


  • Age: Renters must be at least 21 years old. Certain vehicles may require drivers to be 25+.

  • License: A valid Egyptian driver’s license or an international driver’s permit (IDP) is required.

  • Payment: All major credit/debit cards are accepted. A security deposit is required and will be held during the rental period.

  • Free cancellations up to 24 hours before pickup, Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee up to 1 day’s rental.
